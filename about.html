About Me

Why I created this blog

I created this blog as I wanted a place to store all the techy thoughts I have in my brain, it was initially for me to unpack tasks that I have completed in my day job so all my process' and errors I encountered are in a nice format rather than just in my notes app on my phone, although it was quite organized with the features such as bullet check lists and formatting that is available on the notes app, there wasn't any code snippets on there which is arguably the most important part!

Coincidentally around the the time I was starting this blog (I had originally started this blog on wix which crazily enough was harder than coding myself as you can probably tell form the basic styles that you can see, frontend and UX isn't my favorite, so using wix took all the enjoyable aspects such as backend and functionality out of the picture and left me with just the tedious work) I joined the BGT X Monzo mentorship and part of the mentorship was to work on a project with my mentor Amy. After bouncing some ideas around we collectively decided that the blog was the best project to do as I was going to do one anyway and we thought of some features that would make it more interesting and less front end-y which you will be seeing in due course!

My background

I graduated from the University of Kent in 2022 with a Computer Science with A year in Industry degree, I completed my year in industry as at Kent Police as a software developer. I am now currently working as a DevOps engineer and have been since Jan 2023
